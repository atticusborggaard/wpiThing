Construction

How I built it


I started off with some wood
Then it turned into a boat

It's not a boat quite yet, still a little flat

I cut out some side pieces

Then I put it together and I basically had a boat

I added some strengthening chines, and planed everything smooth

This created a ton of shavings


Then I had to turn some large square pieces of wood into octagons to be turned into the circular spars.

from there I gave it twice the amount of sides, making it almost into a circle

Then I sanded each one round, inhaling like a half a pound of sawdust

Next I covered the boat in like 7 or eight layers of various goops. It looked a lot better after

Finally I tied on some rigging and added the sails
